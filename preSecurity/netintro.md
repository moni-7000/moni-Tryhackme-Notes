## What is networking ?

in simple words, just things that are connected.

## And the internet?

- a giant network with other networks (private networks) inside



Networks are either *private* or *public*.

and devices must be identifiable on a network:
- IP address
- Media Access Control (MAC) address


#### IP ADRESSES
IPv4:
- xxx.xxx.xxx.xxx
- 4 groups, each a num 0-225
- ex: 192.168.1.1


*public* IP addresses to identify on the internet, *private* IP addresses to identify amongst other devices.
 
Devices on the same network can use their private IP address to communicate with each other, and anything they send to internet will use the same *public* IP address.

We get our public IP address from our Internet Service Provider at a monthly price (cool!)
